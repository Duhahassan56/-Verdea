Plant Profile

Monstera Deliciosa

Indoor indirect light water weekly Not Pet-friendly
Native to Central America
Growth speed fast
Max height Up to 3m

Names

Botanical name Monstera deliciosa
Common name(s) Swiss cheese plant, Split-leaf philodendron

About the plant

Monstera Deliciosa is a popular indoor plant known for its large, glossy leaves and fast growth. It's loved for its bold look and low maintenance needs.

Care basics

Light

Bright, indirect light

Water

When top soil feels dry

Humidity

Medium to high

Difficulty

Easy

Tips

Rotate the pot every 1-2 weeks for even growth

Wipe leaves to keep them shiny and dust-free

Use a moss pole if you want it to climb

Warnings

Toxic to pets if ingested. Keep away from cats and dogs.
